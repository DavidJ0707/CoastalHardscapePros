Our Services

At Coastal Hardscape Pros, we offer a range of services to transform your outdoor spaces. From custom pavers to concrete work, our expert team is here to bring your vision to life.

We provide custom paver installations to enhance the beauty of your driveways, patios, and walkways. Our pavers are designed to be durable, beautiful, and fit seamlessly into your outdoor space.

Our retaining wall solutions are both functional and stylish, helping to prevent erosion while adding a stunning feature to your landscape.

From fireplaces and outdoor kitchens to pergolas and pavilions, we create outdoor living areas that you and your family can enjoy year-round.

Concrete Work

Whether it's concrete removal or new installations, our team provides professional concrete services to meet your specific needs.

Repair & Maintenance

We also offer cleaning, sealing, and repair services to maintain and protect your hardscape, ensuring it stays beautiful for years to come.

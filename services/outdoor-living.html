Outdoor Living

Expand your living space to the outdoors with our custom solutions. From outdoor kitchens to cozy fireplaces, we help you create a perfect space for relaxation, family gatherings, and entertaining guests.

Create Your Dream Outdoor Living Space

At Coastal Hardscape Pros, we bring your outdoor living dreams to life. From cozy fireplaces to fully-equipped outdoor kitchens, stunning pergolas, and inviting pavilions, we specialize in designing and building outdoor living spaces that are perfect for entertaining, relaxing, and enjoying nature. Our outdoor living solutions are tailored to your needs, enhancing the functionality and beauty of your backyard.

Why Choose Outdoor Living Spaces?

  • Enhanced Lifestyle: Outdoor living areas provide the perfect space for family gatherings, entertaining friends, or simply enjoying a peaceful evening under the stars.
  • Increased Property Value: Professionally designed outdoor living features can significantly boost the value and curb appeal of your home.
  • Customizable Designs: Whether you envision a rustic fireplace, a gourmet outdoor kitchen, or a beautiful pergola, our designs are fully customizable to match your style and preferences.
  • Year-Round Enjoyment: With features like fireplaces and pavilions, you can enjoy your outdoor space throughout the year, regardless of the season.
  • Extended Living Space: Create an extension of your indoor living area, providing more space to relax and make memories with loved ones.

Our Outdoor Living Design and Build Process

Our goal is to create outdoor living spaces that meet your unique needs and vision. Here's how we do it:

  1. Initial Consultation: We start by understanding your vision, evaluating your outdoor space, and discussing ideas that align with your lifestyle.
  2. Custom Design: Our designers create a detailed plan, including 3D renderings if needed, to bring your vision to life and ensure everything fits seamlessly.
  3. Material Selection: We help you choose the best materials for your outdoor living features, ensuring both beauty and durability.
  4. Construction: Our skilled team builds each element of your outdoor living space with precision and care, ensuring top-quality craftsmanship.
  5. Finishing Touches: We complete the project by adding finishing touches, such as lighting, landscaping, and additional details that make your outdoor living area truly special.
